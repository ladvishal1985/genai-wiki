Semantic memory in LLM agents stores general facts, knowledge, and concepts about the world or domain—not specific past events. It is analogous to human declarative knowledge (knowing "Paris is the capital of France" vs. remembering the specific conversation where you learned it). In implementation, semantic memory is typically a vector database of fact chunks retrieved via similarity search. RAG systems are essentially a form of semantic memory architecture.
