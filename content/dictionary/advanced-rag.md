Advanced RAG encompasses improvements beyond naive RAG: pre-retrieval (query rewriting, HyDE, decomposition), retrieval (hybrid search, reranking, multi-query), and post-retrieval (context compression, answer verification, iterative refinement). Advanced RAG techniques address the failure modes of naive RAG—vocabulary gap, chunk boundary issues, context length limits—at the cost of increased latency and complexity.
