Graph RAG augments retrieval with a knowledge graph built from the document corpus. Entities and relationships are extracted and stored as graph nodes and edges. At query time, graph traversal supplements vector search—retrieving not just semantically similar chunks but connected entities and their relationships. Graph RAG handles multi-hop reasoning and thematic summarization better than flat chunk retrieval, but requires significant up-front graph construction cost.
