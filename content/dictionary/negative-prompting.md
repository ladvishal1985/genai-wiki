Negative prompting explicitly instructs the model on what to avoid or exclude in its output. In image generation (Stable Diffusion), negative prompts describe undesired visual elements. In text LLMs, negative prompting lists behaviors or content to omit ("Do not use bullet points", "Do not mention competitors", "Do not add caveats"). While straightforward, negative prompts are often less reliable than positive framing—models are better at "do X" than "don't do Y."
