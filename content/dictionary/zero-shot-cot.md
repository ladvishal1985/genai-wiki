Zero-Shot Chain-of-Thought (Zero-Shot CoT) is the technique of appending "Let's think step by step" (or similar) to a prompt without providing any reasoning examples. Surprisingly, this simple instruction elicits explicit reasoning steps from large models and significantly improves accuracy on multi-step tasks. It is the minimal form of chain-of-thought prompting and a strong baseline before investing in few-shot reasoning examples.
