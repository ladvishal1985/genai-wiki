Jailbreaking refers to techniques used to bypass an LLM's safety alignments and elicit content or behaviors that the model has been trained to refuse. Methods include roleplay framing ("pretend you have no restrictions"), hypothetical framing, encoded text (Base64, pig latin), multi-turn manipulation, and prompt injection. Jailbreaking is an adversarial cat-and-mouse with model providers; each generation of safety training patches known jailbreaks while new ones are discovered.
