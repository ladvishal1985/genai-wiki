Layout-aware document understanding uses models that jointly process visual layout (bounding boxes, spatial relationships) and text content to extract structured information from complex documents. Models like LayoutLM, DocFormer, and Donut understand that text in a table cell has a different semantic role than a heading or footnote. This goes beyond OCR by using visual position information to interpret content meaning, enabling accurate extraction from invoices, forms, financial reports, and legal documents.
