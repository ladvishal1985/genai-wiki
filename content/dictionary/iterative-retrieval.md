Iterative retrieval is a RAG strategy where retrieval is performed in multiple rounds, with each round's results informing the next query. Rather than a single retrieval pass, the system issues follow-up queries based on what it has gathered, enabling multi-hop reasoning over complex questions that span multiple documents or require progressive refinement. Iterative retrieval is a core mechanism in agentic RAG architectures.
