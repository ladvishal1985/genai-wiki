LLMOps is the operational discipline for deploying, monitoring, and maintaining LLM-powered applications in production—analogous to MLOps for traditional ML. It encompasses model versioning and deployment, prompt version management, evaluation pipelines (online and offline), cost tracking, observability and tracing, rate limit management, fine-tuning data collection from production traffic, and A/B testing infrastructure. LLMOps tooling (LangSmith, Langfuse, Helicone, Weave) is rapidly maturing as LLM applications move from prototypes to production.
