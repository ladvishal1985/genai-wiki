Contrastive Language-Image Pretraining (CLIP) is a model from OpenAI trained on 400 million (image, caption) pairs using a contrastive objective: image and text encoders are trained so matching pairs have high cosine similarity while non-matching pairs are pushed apart. CLIP produces aligned image and text embeddings enabling zero-shot image classification and cross-modal retrieval. It is the foundational architecture for most multimodal embedding systems.
