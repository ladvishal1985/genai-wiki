Reasoning tokens (or "thinking tokens") are tokens generated by the model as an extended internal scratchpad before producing the final answer, in models like OpenAI o1 and Anthropic's Claude extended thinking mode. These tokens are usually hidden from the user but represent the model's working memory for multi-step reasoning. The reasoning token budget can be increased for harder problems, trading latency and cost for accuracy. Reasoning tokens enable models to break down complex problems step by step.
