Tracing and observability for LLM applications involves capturing structured logs of every step in an inference pipeline—input, retrieved chunks, intermediate LLM calls, tool outputs, and final response—with latency, cost, and quality metadata. Tools like LangSmith, Langfuse, and Helicone provide trace visualization. Observability is essential for debugging silent failures, detecting quality regressions in production, and building the datasets needed for regression testing and fine-tuning data collection.
